About Voice It Out

Empowering women and girls to speak out against digital violence.

Our Mission

Voice It Out (VIO) is a platform designed to provide survivors with a safe space to report incidents, access resources, and receive support. Built during the Power Hacks Hackathon under the theme “Unite to End Digital Violence Against All Women & Girls”, VIO leverages technology to amplify voices, promote safety by design, and connect survivors to the help they need.

Our Vision

We envision a digital world where women and girls can thrive without fear of harassment, bullying, or violence. By combining empathetic design, strong technical foundations, and AI-powered insights, VIO aims to be a trusted companion for survivors everywhere.
